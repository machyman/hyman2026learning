# Pre-submission checklist

Use this checklist before submitting AI-assisted work. It is the student checklist from Part I of *Learning with AI*.

- [ ] I know the AI policy for this assignment.
- [ ] I made my own first attempt before using AI for substantial help.
- [ ] I used AI for support, not replacement.
- [ ] I checked important claims, sources, calculations, or interpretations.
- [ ] I removed any AI-generated material I could not explain.
- [ ] I protected private, restricted, and confidential information.
- [ ] I disclosed AI use if the assignment required it.
- [ ] I can explain the final work in my own words.
- [ ] I can close AI and my notes and redo the key argument, calculation, proof, or paragraph from a blank page.
- [ ] I know what I learned from the process.

The blank-page item is the strongest single calibration check on the list. Explanation while looking at finished work is easy to confuse with understanding; reconstruction from a blank page is harder to fake.
